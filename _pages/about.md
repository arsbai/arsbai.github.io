    <div>
      <p class="section-heading__kicker">Research statement</p>
      <h2>Sensing Worlds <span>精合感应，知与物接</span></h2>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="intro-grid">
    <p class="intro-grid__lead">LAND denotes the physical world; SCAPE signifies observation and perception. This duality parallels the Chinese context of 景 <em>(physical elements)</em> and 观 <em>(perceiving)</em>.</p>
    <div>
      <p>My research investigates human-environment interactions, from sensory perception of physical and social environments to cognitive-affective processing and resulting behaviors. These experiences provide essential evidence for evaluating environmental quality.</p>
      <p>Through multimodal data, intelligent technologies, and advanced geospatial algorithms, I measure human perception, identify the environmental mechanisms behind perceptual differences, and reveal their deeper geographical and social contexts.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
